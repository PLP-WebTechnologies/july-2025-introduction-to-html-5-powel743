Project Overview

RePET is a student-led initiative focused on reducing plastic waste by upcycling PET bottles into polyester fiber. The project promotes sustainability, innovation, and circular economy solutions.

Our Goals

Reduce Plastic Waste

We aim to minimize the harmful effects of plastic bottles by transforming them into useful products.

Promote Sustainability

By creating polyester fiber from waste, we demonstrate how innovation can support eco-friendly practices.

Create Opportunities

The project also creates job opportunities and encourages communities to take part in responsible waste management.

Our Impact

Through RePET, we plan to recycle thousands of plastic bottles each month, reducing pollution while producing sustainable fiber for textiles and other industries.
